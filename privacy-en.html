Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026 · Gorillax mobile application

1. Data controller

Gorillax is published by Arthur Destribats (« we », « the publisher »). Privacy inquiries: arthur.destribats@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

We collect only data necessary to operate the app:

  • Account: username, email, password (hashed), optional profile photo.
  • Fitness activity: workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weights, durations, goals and stats you enter.
  • Social: posts, likes, messages, challenges and interactions you create.
  • Subscription: Premium status via RevenueCat / Apple (no access to payment card data).
  • Technical: device tokens for push notifications, limited technical logs (errors, security).

We do not use Apple Health / HealthKit in the current version of the app.

3. Purposes

  • Account creation and management
  • Sport, social and messaging features
  • Premium subscription management
  • Security, fraud prevention and user support
  • Service improvement (aggregated analytics, no sale of personal data)

4. Processors

  • Render — API hosting
  • Supabase — database and file storage
  • RevenueCat — in-app subscriptions
  • Apple — App Store distribution and IAP
  • Resend — transactional email
  • Sentry (if enabled) — error monitoring

5. Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. After deletion, we erase or anonymize data within a reasonable period, except where law requires retention.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

EU residents may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and portability at arthur.destribats@gmail.com. You may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

7. Security

We use appropriate measures (HTTPS, password hashing, auth tokens, restricted access). No system is 100% secure.

8. Children

The app is not intended for children under 16. Contact us to delete a minor's data.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The date at the top will be revised. Material changes will be communicated in-app when feasible.
