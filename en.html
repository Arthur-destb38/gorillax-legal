FAQ

Everything you're wondering

Is it really free?

Yes. Workout tracking, programs, history, challenges and the feed are free, with no ads. An optional subscription (Gorillax+) unlocks extras to support the project.

Does it work offline?

Yes — everything works offline: track your workout in the deepest gym basement. Sync resumes automatically as soon as you're back online.

Is it available on Android?

Gorillax is available on iPhone (App Store). The Android version is in the works.

What about my data?

Your training data is yours: no reselling, no targeted ads. Details in the privacy policy.
